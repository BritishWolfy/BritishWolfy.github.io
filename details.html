Project Details

Below are the details on the project, and what will be done to the unit.

Purchase and transfer

The unit will first have to be purchased from the reqiured companies, with funds raised via our GoFundMe page. It will then be transported to a storage facility where restoration can start. Please note that we do not currently have a storage facility, and suggestions via our contact methods would be appreciated.

Restoration

The train will then see full restoration back into it's Network SouthEast specification, with it's original name "Special Olympics" being reapplied. A new motor coach will have to be sourced, either through conversion of an existing TSO, or through purchase of one from another unit. This will allow the train to start running under it's own power. From this, the unit will then see extensive interior refurbishment back into it's original state.
